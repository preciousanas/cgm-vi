WHO WE ARE

We are a ministry mandated by God to build His people into leadership. We believe in the baptism of the Holy Spirit and the manifestation of spiritual gifts.

WHAT WE DO

Our core passion is to evangelise the lost for their total recovery: spirit, soul and body. All these we do throug the various instrument we've deplored. We earnestly desire to make all men see the goodness of God.
